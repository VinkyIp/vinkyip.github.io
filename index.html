Intro

I recently was at TopFunnel for over a year. I joined as the 4th full-time hire handling the Operations team. It was a big jump for me at the time coming from a HR-related mid-sized startup to a really small startup in the recruiting space but I enjoy challenges and I loved opportunity to build not only a product from scratch but a remote team as well. I helped revamp a core feature of the product that allowed our team to handle 3x the number of tasks that came in from customers.

Before TopFunnel, I was a content reviewer at GuideSpark and really liked cross-collaborating with sales, designers, writers on these massive video libraries for Fortune 500 companies. During one of our busiest quarter, my team of 3 worked on over 1,500 update-title short videos (videos that were already created but either needed to be overhauled or updated with new content) which serviced 70% of customer base. My best experience working there is definitely working with like-minded individuals and working towards something great keep our customers happy.

Right now, I’m looking for a data analyst role to utilize my passion for building products through data-driven decisions. I love to take information whether it’s user feedback or trends and collaborate with engineering and product team to create new future-oriented products and helping them scale.
